MEMBERS

Blackpink is a four member K-pop girl group under YG Entertainment.

Comprised of members Jennie, Jisoo, Lisa and Rosé, the group debuted in 2016 with the song “Boombayah."

The song reached No. 1 on the Billboard world digital song sales chart.
